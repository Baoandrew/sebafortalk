Bring a large pot of water to a boil. While the water heats, whisk 3 tablespoons of white miso paste with a ladle of hot broth in a small bowl until smooth — this prevents lumps when added to the soup. Heat the broth gently in a saucepan, then stir in the miso mixture. Do not boil after adding miso — high heat destroys the delicate flavor and aroma.

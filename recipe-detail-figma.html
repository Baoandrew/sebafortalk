A simple ramen recipe with rich miso broth, fresh noodles, soft-boiled egg and classic toppings.

Ingredients

6 items
Fresh ramen noodles
White miso paste
Chicken or pork broth
Soft-boiled eggs
Chashu pork
Scallion and nori

Instructions

1
Prepare the Broth

Warm the broth and mix in miso paste until smooth.

2
Cook the Noodles

Boil noodles until springy, then drain and place in bowls.

3
Assemble and Serve

Add broth, egg, pork, scallion and nori. Serve hot.

Quick Tips

Do not boil miso after adding it.
Use fresh noodles when possible.
Serve immediately for best texture.
